How It Works

Four simple steps from sign-up to silverware.

1

Sign Up

Members receive a notification and enter the tournament with one tap.

2

Draw

Secretary closes entries. The system generates randomized brackets and notifies all players.

3

Play

Advance

Winner reports the score. The system updates the bracket and notifies the next opponent.
